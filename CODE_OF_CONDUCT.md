# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge
We pledge to make participation in our project a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our Standards
Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include respect and collaboration.

## Enforcement Responsibilities
Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing standards.

## Scope
This Code of Conduct applies within project spaces and public spaces.

## Enforcement
Instances of abusive behavior may be reported and reviewed.

## Enforcement Guidelines
Corrective actions may be taken depending on severity.

## Attribution
This Code of Conduct is adapted from the Contributor Covenant.
